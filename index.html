The conditions — 02_Soil

The organizations that thrive through recessions, trade wars, pandemics, and tech shifts are the ones that build communities. Not bigger audiences. Communities. A specifically valuable group of people who share your mission, values, or interests — and who, when meaningfully engaged and empowered, create benefit for everyone in it. That doesn't happen by accident. Community building is not a side project. Community has to be designed.
