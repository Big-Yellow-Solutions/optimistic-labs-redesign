FAQ
FAQ accordion · .faq / details+summary
What exactly is a "lab"?
A lab is a productized practice serving a specific community or vertical. It has a clear methodology, packaged offerings and tiers, go-to-market support, co-branding, and Optimistic Labs' leadership and infrastructure behind it.
Do I keep ownership of my brand and my community?
Yes. Your name, your voice, and your relationships stay yours and stay front and center.
What do I actually get from Optimistic Labs?
Strategy and infrastructure behind you; a trusted network of operators, creators, and builders already in motion; help productizing and pricing your offerings.
